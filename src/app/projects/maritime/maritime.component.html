Maritime simulation

Features

  • Environment
    • Uniform and graded buoyancy forces to model for fluid density interfaces.
    • Modeling of hydrodynamic forces for underwater vessels, including added mass, water currents, linear and quadratic damping, and the coriolis effect.
    • Digital Elevation Maps (DEM) support to load seafloor terrain geometries.
    • Surface wave fields support
  • Actuators
    • Mechanical bladder as buoyancy engine in underwater vessels.
    • Thruster for ships and underwater vessels.
  • Sensors
    • Generic Doppler Velocity Logger (DVL) with bottom and water-mass tracking support. Beam arrangements are fully configurable.
    • Environmental sensors for arbitrary quantities, interpolated in space and time from user-provided data frames.
  • Communications
    • Acoustic communications system, modeling packet collision and drops.

Models

  • Vessels (WAM-V, A, B, C, D, E, F, G)
  • AUVs / ROVs (LRAUV, ROV)
